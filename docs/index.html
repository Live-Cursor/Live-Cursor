Why Live Cursor?

⚡

Zero-Configuration Daemon

No terminal commands or external database systems needed. Live Cursor boots its SQLite WebSocket sync engine in the background with a single toggle.

🔒

100% Privacy-First

Your database files and SQLite assets run entirely on your own local device. Share access with teammates securely without utilizing third-party clouds.

🚀

Conflict-Free Typing

Utilizes advanced real-time operational synchronization algorithms to ensure keystrokes merge instantly without text duplication or overlaps.

🔄

Full Vault Sync

Sync more than just markdown files. Automatically push and pull active vault configurations, active themes, community plugins, and workspace layouts.

How it works

Step 01

Spawn Local Engine

The Obsidian plugin spawns a background Node daemon on your host port 1234 securely.

Step 02

Configure Admin Token

Initialize your administrator credentials in Obsidian settings with zero external tools.

Step 03

Invite Teammates

Generate one-click collaborator keys, share the sync link, and collaborate in real-time!
